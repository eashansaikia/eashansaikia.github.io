Fight for social and environmental justice

Humane Warriors

Humane Warriors is a Switzerland based NGO, formed during the Covid Lockdown. We raised $41,000 and supported relief projects across 18 towns and villages across the country. Currently, our projects focus on building post-corona resilient communities via sustainable education and livelihood projects
