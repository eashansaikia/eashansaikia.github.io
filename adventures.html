Cycling along the River Rhine

In the summer of 2020, I embarked on a solo cycling tour along the Rhine river from its source in Switzerland, to the North Sea in Netherlands. This was a quest to find out if my PhD experience could be extended in the general context of life as it is. In my PhD, I learnt to break up a big problem into a set of small fundamental tasks to be solved everyday for a period of three years. As such, I was curious to see if I can train my mind to focus on 80 kms everyday for a two weeks, rather than a scary 1200 kms.

Additionally, I am clubbing my cycle tour with a fundraiser to build an e-learning center in India, in collaboration with Humane Warriors. Here, I am going to send you a postcard, and share with you a piece of my journey, in return for your contribution to the e-learning project.
